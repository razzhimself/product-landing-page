Wood

A set of --- Wood Rings provides a solid foundation for gymnastic strength building and beginner-to-advanced suspension training. With a textured, grip-friendly surface that works with or without chalk, wood rings have a distinct, natural feel preferred by many athletes. Rogue Wood Rings are also easy to adjust and built with American craftsmanship for lasting durability and comfort.

Steel

heavy-gauge steel gym rings are backed by a lifetime warranty against breaking. Steel Rings feature 1.25" 14-gauge steel tubing, fully TIG welded by hand and powder-coated in a multi-step process with a zinc phosphate plating underneath and a firm but grip-friendly exterior coat. The provided ring straps and buckle system are ultra-durable, easily adjustable, and environmentally versatile. there are very few limitations on where a set of Rogue Rings can go.
