A set of rings provide a full suspension training system for bodyweight fitness with true gymnastic capabilities.
More challenging than a pull-up bar, gym rings can dramatically improve any athlete’s upper body and core strength,
while training better joint alignment and overall balance. Rings are scalable for non-gymnasts but built to
Olympic quality to handle ring dips, muscle-ups, back levers, front levers, pull-ups, dips, ring push ups, ring rows,
and anything else you can throw at them.

Safety

There's no point in going to training to injure yourself,
each set of wooden rings comes with
heavy-duty 1.5” wide nylon ring straps and buckle system,
enabling a quick set-up and easy adjustments, indoors or out.
Velcro fasteners are also provided to keep the excess portion of each strap secured.
The ring straps are available in your choice of black, desert tan, or ranger green,
and can be hung from a crossmember in your gym or by a swingset, secure tree branch, etc.
